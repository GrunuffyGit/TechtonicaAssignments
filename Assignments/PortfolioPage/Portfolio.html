About Me

Hello! My name is Gloria. A little background of me is that I used to be a manual QA tester. I took what developers created and get to try to break it, which was really fun. I got to work closely with them and thought what they were doing cool. It's exiciting to create something out of nothing. It's kind of like magic! I dabbled a little bit into coding in school, such as intro courses, but I never really had the opertunity to just focus on it. I was working a full time job and after work, I would be in school trying to earn a degree (unrelated to tech). I was sort of in a runt, until my sister told me about Techtonica, a free coding bootcamp for women and binary adults. I'm grateful for this oppertunity because I can put all my focus on learning to become a full stack software developer without having to worry about finding resources and other living factors, such as a laptop to work on, meals being provided, and etc. I hope in the future, I can give back and contribute to the community while doing what I enjoy.
